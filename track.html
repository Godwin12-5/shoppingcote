How to track your order

Tracking your order on jumia is easy and simple. Here's how to do it.

Step 1:

Log in to your account

Step 2:

Click on the "ORDER"

Step 3:

Find the order you want to track and click on"See Details".

Step 4:

On the order details page, click on track my item to view the currrent status and a complete tracking history.

Alternatively once your oder is shipped, you can also track your your order by using your "package number" provided i n your shipping email. Simply enter the package number into jumia's PACKAGE TRACKING PAGEto view the lestest updates on your packages.
